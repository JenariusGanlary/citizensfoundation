# Citizens Foundation Design System

# Design Philosophy

The website should communicate trust, measurable impact, sustainability, and innovation.

It should feel premium, modern, and human—not like a traditional NGO website.

Our design language is inspired by:

- Apple (Typography & Whitespace)
- Stripe (Layout & Polish)
- Charity: Water (Storytelling)
- National Geographic (Photography)
